Sur Ron Mod Guide

The beginner’s guide to modding the Sur Ron Light Bee X. This guide is specific for the Sur Ron Light Bee X, and the Segway electric bikes. There are so many mods that can be done to these bikes. They can be modded to suit your ridding terrain, and ridding style.

Sur Ron Light Bee X

The Sur Ron Light Bee X is sold as a lightweight off road electric bike. It is sold by Lunacycle for about $4,400. The bike weighs about 120 U.S. pounds, has a top speed of about 45 miles per hour, and has a range of about 30 to 50 miles. The battery takes 3 hours to fully recharge when it’s fully drained.
